Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To see the nations of the earth transformed by the power of the Gospel and the manifestation of God's Kingdom in every sphere of society.

Our Mission

To raise, equip, and deploy Kingdom ambassadors globally through prayer, revival, discipleship, and leadership development.
