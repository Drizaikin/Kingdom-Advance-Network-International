About KANI

Discover the vision, mission, and heartbeat of our global kingdom movement.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To see the nations of the earth transformed by the power of the Gospel and the manifestation of God's Kingdom in every sphere of society.

Our Mission

To raise, equip, and deploy Kingdom ambassadors globally through prayer, revival, discipleship, and leadership development.

Core Values

🔥

Passion for God

We prioritize intimacy with God through prayer and worship.

📖

Word-Based

Our foundation is the infallible Word of God.

🌍

Global Impact

We are called to reach the unreached across the nations.

⭐

Excellence

We reflect God's nature through excellence in all we do.
