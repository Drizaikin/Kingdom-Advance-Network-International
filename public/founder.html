Biography

With over two decades of ministry experience, the founder of KANI has traveled extensively, preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom with undeniable demonstrations of God's power. His passion is to see believers walk in their full potential and take their rightful place as kings and priests on the earth.

Through dynamic teaching, prophetic insight, and a relentless pursuit of God's presence, he has trained thousands of leaders who are now making significant impact in their respective spheres of influence.
